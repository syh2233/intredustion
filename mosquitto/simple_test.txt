简单的测试命令:

1. 启动Mosquitto:
   mosquitto.exe -c mosquitto_simple.conf -v

2. 测试发布消息:
   mosquitto_pub.exe -h 192.168.24.23 -t test/simple -m "Hello"

3. 测试订阅消息:
   mosquitto_sub.exe -h 192.168.24.23 -t test/simple -v

4. 监控ESP32数据:
   mosquitto_sub.exe -h 192.168.24.23 -t "esp32/#" -v

5. 检查端口监听:
   netstat -an | findstr :1883

6. 检查进程:
   tasklist | findstr mosquitto
